Moldy Melons: Where the people can speak!

"Why are we a thing?"

I don't know about you, but the team over at MM is sick and tired of letting critics make or break a movie, game, or book. After all, critics are people like you and me. But with that being said, who's to say that a critic's voice has more power than the average joes out there? Not anymore, thanks to Moldy Melons! Now, by joining our page, you can give honest reviews that other people like you can see before investing in that given movie, book, or game.

So... What the heck are you waiting for? Sign up now!
